Some information about us

 Rafting is a practice of descent in rapids in the team that uses inflatable boats and safety equipment. During the tracking or interaction group in the midst of nature, encountering challenges that provide the same agreement with the team spirit. Commercial rafting provides the experience of going down the river for people of any age and most people who have never had a previous experience, using accessible sport.

 Our guides have professional experiences from 2 to 13 years, where they experience various types of practitioners, from children from 3 years of age to adolescents of 85 years, with 4 generations of the same family. Providing enjoyable activities for the family and radical activities for the more adventurous.

  We have a skilled team of instructors who are highly qualified to carry out the kayaking activity, our instructors are trained to serve all types of people, from the most experienced to the beginner.

 we have a variety of sizes of rafts. Rafting is an enjoyable, relaxing, safe means of floating.
