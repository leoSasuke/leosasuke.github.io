Target Audience

The purpose of this site is to inform the climatic conditions of the coast of Espirito Santo,
for people who like to do some type of activity on the beach.

Persona 1

Leonardo likes to gather his friends on the beach to have a barbecue and swim for a while, with the access to the "my-beach-funny" website throught you cellphone and sometimes tablet, he manages to gather the necessary information, to gather the crowd, there he finds information like the force of the wind and if the tide is full.

Persona 2

Leandro is a father of twins, 35 years old and before planning any type of outdoor activity on the beach, he accesses the "my-beach-funny" website trhought your cellphone and sometimes your PC, to know the weather conditions and thus plan an activity that is fun and with safety.

Scenario 1

How far out does the site forecast?

Our goal is to give accurate current weather conditions and provide a accurate forecast allowing for you to plan your activities later in the week.

Scenario 2

can I get some kind of service in addition to the weather?

yes, we also inform you of the conditions of the roads that give access to the desired location.

Scenario 3

Can I send the current weather to a friend and how many different ways can I send it?

Our website goal to make weather social as well. Our site will let you send the current weather conditions as well as the 7 day forcast to anyone you want. You will be able to send it via text message and email.

Scenario 4

Can you find out the nearest locations in real time?

yes, our system tells you how many people are preparing an activity on site.

Scenario 5

If I don't have a means of trasport can I rent a car through the website?

No, this kind of service we don't do, but we can send information about the bus.
